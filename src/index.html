Frequently Asked Questions

Do you also design mobile applications?

Of course! Mobile and web applications are definitely in our portfolio. Lack of responsiveness these days is unthinkable. Each design is created by default for all common screen sizes.

How many fixes are included?

We provide a maximum of 3 corrections for the basic plan. Each subsequent revision after approval of the project is an additional cost of 30% of the basic price.

How long is the waiting time for the project?

It all depends on the size of the project, but we always try to keep the waiting time as short as possible, for large projects it is a maximum of 3 months.

Does the cooperation include marketing activities?

Full-scope marketing activities are available in the Extended Care Package. It is always cooperation with an external company focused on marketing activities in the strict sense.
